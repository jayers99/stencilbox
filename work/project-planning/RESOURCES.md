## ğŸ“– Essential Reading

### Books on Technical Design & Documentation

1. **Continuous Delivery** by Jez Humble & Dave Farley
   - The foundational text on CD practices
   - Covers configuration management, continuous integration, testing, and deployment

2. **Building Evolutionary Architectures** by Neal Ford, Rebecca Parsons, Patrick Kua
   - ThoughtWorks authors on adaptable system design
   - Fitness functions for architecture governance

3. **Software Architecture: The Hard Parts** by Neal Ford, Mark Richards, et al.
   - Modern distributed architecture trade-offs
   - Decision-making frameworks

4. **Domain-Driven Design** by Eric Evans
   - Strategic design and bounded contexts
   - Ubiquitous language (relevant for SOD Glossary section)

5. **The DevOps Handbook** by Gene Kim, Jez Humble, et al.
   - End-to-end DevOps practices
   - Case studies from high-performing organizations

6. **Team Topologies** by Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais
   - Organizing teams for fast flow
   - Conway's Law and system design
